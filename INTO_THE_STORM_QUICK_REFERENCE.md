# Into the Storm Homeworlds - Quick Reference

A quick reference for the 6 new homeworlds from Into the Storm supplement.

---

## Frontier World (p. 10)

**Replaces:** Death World  
**Characteristics:** +5 Str, −5 Int  
**Wounds:** 2×TB + 1d5 + 2  
**Fate:** 1d10: 1-5 = 2 FP, 6-10 = 3 FP  

**Skills:**
- Survival (Trained)
- Wrangling (Trained)

**Abilities:**
- **Tough as Grox-Hide:** +1 Wound
- **Leery of Outsiders:** −10 Fellowship with strangers
- **Tenacious Survivalist:** Re-roll Initiative (must accept second)
- **Xenos Interaction:** Immune to Fear (1-2) from xenos, −5 with Imperial Cult

**Flavor:** Rough-and-tumble settlers on lawless border worlds who deal with xenos and survive harsh conditions.

---

## Footfallen (p. 11)

**Replaces:** Void Born  
**Characteristics:** −5 BS, −5 T, +5 Ag, +5 Fel  
**Wounds:** 2×TB + 1d5  
**Fate:** 1d10: 1-4 = 2 FP, 5-7 = 3 FP, 8-10 = 4 FP  

**Skills:**
- Common Lore (Koronus Expanse) (Trained)

**Abilities:**
- **Street Knowledge:** −5 Scholastic Lore (except Koronus Expanse)
- **Web of Contacts:** Choose Peer Talent (Astropaths, Insane, Underworld, Void Born, or Workers)
- **Port of Call:** Gain Polyglot
- **Sixth Sense:** Gain Psyniscience (not a psyker) + Rival (Inquisition)

**Flavor:** Cunning dealers and information brokers from the settlements of the Koronus Expanse.

---

## Fortress World (p. 12)

**Replaces:** Forge World  
**Characteristics:** +5 BS, +5 WP, −5 Int, −5 Fel  
**Wounds:** 2×TB + 1d5 + 1  
**Fate:** 1d10: 1-9 = 3 FP, 10 = 4 FP  

**Skills:**
- Secret Tongue (Military) (Trained)
- Common Lore (War) (Trained)

**Abilities:**
- **Hated Enemy:** Choose Hatred talent for fortress world's enemy
- **Constant Combat Training:** Choose Basic Weapon Training (Las or SP), −5 social on non-combat topics
- **Steel Nerve:** Gain Nerves of Steel

**Flavor:** Disciplined military defenders trained from birth to fight the Imperium's enemies.

---

## Battlefleet (p. 13)

**Replaces:** Hive World  
**Characteristics:** +5 WP, +3 Fel, −5 Per  
**Wounds:** 2×TB + 1d5  
**Fate:** 1d10: 1-6 = 3 FP, 7-10 = 4 FP  

**Skills:**
- Common Lore (Imperial Navy) (Trained)
- Speak Language (Battlefleet War Cant) (Trained)

**Abilities:**
- **Officer on Deck:** +5 Command aboard spacecraft
- **Void-Born Ancestry:** Choose Navigation (Stellar) or Pilot (Space Craft), gain Void Accustomed
- **Ship-Bound Fighter:** −2 Initiative and double Long Range penalty planetside (unless confined space)

**Flavor:** Proud naval officers from the Imperial Battlefleet with strong sense of duty and honor.

---

## Penal World (p. 14)

**Replaces:** Imperial World  
**Characteristics:** −5 WP, +5 Per, +5 T, −5 Fel  
**Wounds:** 2×TB + 1d5 + 1  
**Fate:** 1d10: 1-6 = 2 FP, 7-9 = 3 FP, 10 = 4 FP  

**Skills:**
- Security (Untrained Basic)
- **Choice:** Deceive OR Intimidate (Trained)

**Abilities:**
- **Syndicate:** Gain Peer (Underworld)
- **Criminal:** −20 Interaction with authorities if origin known
- **Nightmares:** Gain 1d5 Insanity + Light Sleeper
- **Underground Resources:** Once per two sessions, improve Availability via criminal contacts

**Flavor:** Hardened survivors from brutal prison worlds who are resourceful but marked as criminals.

---

## Child of Dynasty (p. 15-16)

**Replaces:** Noble Born  
**Characteristics:** −3 T, +3 Int, −5 WP, +5 Fel  
**Wounds:** 2×TB + 1d5  
**Fate:** 1d10: 1-3 = 2 FP, 4-7 = 3 FP, 8-10 = 4 FP  

**Skills:**
- Literacy (Trained)
- Speak Language (High Gothic) (Trained)

**Abilities:**
- **Dynastic Warrant:** +3 Ship Points (cannot convert to Profit Factor)
- **Honour Amongst One's Peers:** +5 Fellowship with officials/nobility in formal settings
- **Unseen Enemy:** Gain Enemy (unseen foe determined by GM)

**Flavor:** Heirs to powerful Rogue Trader dynasties with wealth, training, and dangerous rivals.

## Usage Notes

### When to Choose Each:

**Frontier World** - Tough survivalists with xenos experience  
**Footfallen** - Social characters who know the Expanse  
**Fortress World** - Combat-focused military characters  
**Battlefleet** - Void-based officers and commanders  
**Penal World** - Gritty survivors with underworld connections  
**Child of Dynasty** - Wealthy heirs with political connections  

### System Integration:

All homeworlds are fully integrated with:
- Wound/Fate formulas for automatic calculation
- Talent grants system for cascading abilities
- Proper modifier structures
- Source references for rulebook lookup

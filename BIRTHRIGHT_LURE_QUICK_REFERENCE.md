# Birthright & Lure of the Void - Quick Reference

**Character Creation Step 2 & 3** | Rogue Trader Core Rulebook pp. 28-31

---

## Birthright (Step 2)

Your early life circumstances and formative experiences.

### Scavenger (Page 28)
**Theme:** Survival on society's fringes  
**Choices:**
- +3 Willpower OR +3 Agility
- Unremarkable Talent OR Resistance (Fear) Talent
- 1d5 Corruption OR 1d5 Insanity

### Scapegrace (Page 29)
**Theme:** Life as a ne'er-do-well on the borderlands  
**Gains:**
- Sleight of Hand (trained skill)
- +3 Intelligence OR +3 Perception
- 1d5 Corruption OR 1d5 Insanity

### Stubjack (Page 29)
**Theme:** Mercenary warrior born to violence  
**Gains:**
- Quick Draw Talent
- Intimidate (trained skill)
- +5 Weapon Skill OR +5 Ballistic Skill
**Penalties:**
- −5 Fellowship
- 1d5 Insanity Points

### Child of the Creed (Page 29)
**Theme:** Raised in the Ministorum's embrace  
**Gains:**
- Unshakeable Faith Talent
- +3 Willpower OR +3 Fellowship
**Penalties:**
- −3 Weapon Skill

### Savant (Page 29)
**Theme:** Scholar and keeper of knowledge  
**Choices:**
- Logic (Int) trained skill OR Peer (Academic) Talent
- +3 Intelligence OR +3 Fellowship
**Penalties:**
- −3 Toughness

### Vaunted (Page 29)
**Theme:** Born to wealth and privilege  
**Gains:**
- Decadence Talent
- +3 Agility OR +3 Fellowship
**Penalties:**
- −3 Perception
- 1d5 Corruption Points

## Lure of the Void (Step 3)

What drew you to the stars and away from your homeworld.

### Tainted (Page 30)
**Theme:** Marked as outcast or heretic  
**Choose One Path:**

1. **Mutant**
   - Roll once on Mutations table (p. 369)
   - OR spend 200 xp to choose result <76

2. **Insane**
   - +3 Toughness
   - Peer (The Insane) Talent
   - 2d10 Insanity Points
   - −3 Fellowship OR −1 Fate Point

3. **Deviant Philosophy**
   - +3 Willpower
   - Enemy (Ecclesiarchy) Talent

### Criminal (Page 30)
**Theme:** Fleeing justice or the underworld  
**Choose One Path:**

1. **Wanted Fugitive**
   - Enemy (Adeptus Arbites) Talent
   - Peer (Underworld) Talent

2. **Hunted by a Crime Baron**
   - +3 Perception
   - Enemy (Underworld) Talent

3. **Judged and Found Wanting**
   - −5 Fellowship
   - Poor bionic limb/implant (200 xp → common, 300 xp → good)

### Renegade (Page 30)
**Theme:** Rebel against Imperial order  
**Choose One Path:**

1. **Recidivist**
   - Enemy (Adeptus Arbites) Talent
   - Resistance (Interrogation) Talent
   - Concealment (trained skill)

2. **Free-thinker**
   - +3 Intelligence OR +3 Perception
   - −3 Willpower
   - Enemy (Ecclesiarchy) Talent

3. **Dark Visionary**
   - Dark Soul Talent
   - Forbidden Lore (choose one) trained skill
   - 1d5+1 Corruption OR 1d5+1 Insanity Points

### Duty Bound (Page 31)
**Theme:** Called to service by faith or honor  
**Choose One Path:**

1. **Duty to the Throne**
   - +3 Willpower
   - Armour of Contempt Talent (if WP 40+)
   - −10 to Interaction Skills vs. non-Imperium

2. **Duty to Humanity**
   - +3 Perception OR +3 Intelligence
   - −1 starting Profit Factor

3. **Duty to Your Dynasty**
   - Rival (Rogue Trader family) Talent
   - −3 Toughness
   - +1 starting Profit Factor

### Zealot (Page 31)
**Theme:** Faith that drives you to the stars  
**Choose One Path:**

1. **Blessed Scars**
   - +10 to Intimidate Tests
   - −10 to Charm Tests
   - Poor bionic limb/implant (200 xp → common, 300 xp → good)

2. **Unnerving Clarity**
   - +5 Willpower
   - −5 Fellowship OR 1d10 Insanity Points

3. **Favoured of the Faithful**
   - +5 Fellowship
   - Peer (Ecclesiarchy) Talent
   - −5 Toughness

### Chosen by Destiny (Page 31)
**Theme:** Certain of your own greatness  
**Choose One Path:**

1. **Seeker of Truth**
   - Foresight Talent
   - Enemy (Academics OR Ecclesiarchy) Talent
   - −3 Willpower

2. **Xenophile**
   - +10 Fellowship vs. alien races/cultures
   - −5 Willpower vs. alien artefacts/psychic powers

3. **Fated for Greatness**
   - +1 Fate Point
   - 1d10+1 Insanity Points

## Quick Selection Tips

### For Combat Characters
- **Stubjack** (Birthright) → WS/BS +5, Quick Draw
- **Duty to the Throne** (Lure) → WP +3, Armour of Contempt

### For Social Characters
- **Vaunted** (Birthright) → Fellowship +3, Decadence
- **Zealot: Favoured** (Lure) → Fellowship +5, Peer (Ecclesiarchy)

### For Skill Specialists
- **Scapegrace** (Birthright) → Sleight of Hand, Perception +3
- **Renegade: Recidivist** (Lure) → Concealment, resistance to interrogation

### For Psykers/Mystics
- **Child of the Creed** (Birthright) → Unshakeable Faith, Willpower +3
- **Chosen: Seeker** (Lure) → Foresight

### For Profit Factor Impact
- **Duty to Humanity** → −1 Profit Factor
- **Duty to Dynasty** → +1 Profit Factor

### For High Fate
- **Chosen: Fated for Greatness** → +1 Fate Point (but 1d10+1 Insanity)

---

## Common Combinations

### The Faithful Warrior
- Child of the Creed (Birthright) → Unshakeable Faith
- Zealot (Lure) → Enhanced faith benefits

### The Hardened Survivor
- Scavenger (Birthright) → Resistance (Fear)
- Tainted: Insane (Lure) → Peer (The Insane), more toughness

### The Criminal Outcast
- Scapegrace (Birthright) → Sleight of Hand
- Criminal: Wanted Fugitive (Lure) → Peer (Underworld)

### The Noble Renegade
- Vaunted (Birthright) → High Fellowship
- Renegade: Free-thinker (Lure) → Intelligence, but Enemy (Ecclesiarchy)

### The Scholarly Mystic
- Savant (Birthright) → Logic/Intelligence
- Chosen: Seeker (Lure) → Foresight

---

## Notes

- **Corruption/Insanity:** Several options grant random Corruption or Insanity Points (roll dice as specified)
- **Bionics:** Some options grant poor-quality bionics that can be upgraded with XP
- **Enemies:** Multiple options grant Enemy talents (Arbites, Ecclesiarchy, Underworld, etc.)
- **Profit Factor:** Only Duty Bound paths affect group Profit Factor
- **Fate Points:** Only "Fated for Greatness" and "Tainted: Insane" affect starting Fate
- **XP Costs:** Some upgrades (bionic quality, mutation selection) require spending starting XP
